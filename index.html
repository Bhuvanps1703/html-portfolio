Avocado Toast Recipe

Ingredients

For Seasoning

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Optional toppings:

  • cherry tomatoes
  • red pepper flakes
  • poached egg
  • feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Toast the slices of whole-grain bread to your liking.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the ripe avocado in half and remove the pit. Scoop the avocado into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork and add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
  5. Add your choice of optional toppings, such as sliced cherry etc...
  6. Enjoy your quick and delicious avocado toast!
